The Designer


Design has been my career option since I have any sort of memory. I remember spending hours with my crayons and papers across kindergarten. Later on in high school I was embraced by photography and my career plan started to be developed. I ended up attending the Norwalk C. College to study graphic design and then the University of Connecticut to digital media. I am always trying to learn some technique or new software that can expand my creativity. Shoot me an idea and I will be glad to polish it.
